About Riwayat

Riwayat is an inter-pool competition being organized this summer exclusively for all Y20s, be it UG or PG. The tournament will be contested by 6 competing pools consisting of UGs of Halls 2, 3, 5, 6, 12 and PG-Y20. There will be numerous events organised and conducted by various clubs and cells spread over different councils of students' gymkhana. The pool with the maximum overall points at the end of riwayat will be declared the champions!

Note: Site contains info of events by cells only as of now. Other events would be updated soon!

Points per position in each event

1st  position      100% points
2nd position      70% points
3rd position       45% points
4th position       30% points
5th position       20% points
6th position       10% points

Points Distribution

Policymakers :                               12.5%

Fake News/Satire Writing :          10%
Survey Design & Data Analysis : 10%
Comics :                                           5%
Speak in your language :              10%
Case Study Challenge :                 15%
Designathon :                                 25%

Events' Details

Competitions are being conducted over a span of 3 months, June to August.

  • Vox Populi
  • PPOC
  • E-Cell
  • CWC

For queries related to Vox Populi, contact the chief editors of Vox Populi,
Ankur Banga and Aditya Raghav Trivedi

01

Fake news / Satire writing

An exciting competition to test your creativity to deploy wit, irony, sarcasm, and ridicule in criticizing faults. Participants need to construct a composed piece of around 500-600 words individually or in a group of 2-4 people.

Details
02

Survey design and data analysis

Participants would have to complete exploratory and analytical tasks based on the data. You will be provided with two documents:
1. The pdf of the survey questionnaire
2. Raw data of the responses received.

Details
03

Comics

This one is for all the comic lovers out there. Use your artistry to create a comic on the following topics:
Types of people in an online class
Rest of the comic ideas can be taken from satire/fake news part only.

Details

For queries related to Public Policy and Opinion Cell, contact the Overall Coordinators,
Aditya Krishna Rao and Nikhil Kumar Singh

01

Policymakers

'Policymakers' is a competition where you get a chance to showcase your analytical abilities in identifying issues pertaining to our society and providing a solution to them in the form of a policy brief.

Details
02

Sansad

'SANSAD' is a 2-day event-cum-competition envisioned on the lines of Youth Parliament, where participants shall get involved in various proceedings of the House, including the Discussion Hour and the Question Hour, all centred upon a pre-determined motion.

Details

For queries related to E-Cell, contact the Overall Coordinators and managers,
Khushboo Sachdeva , Shruthi Sureshkumar and Acanksha Jain

01

Designathon

Designathon is a product design competition, where you get an opportunity to find a problem, build a solution, design a product prototype and pitch your idea to esteemed judges.

Details

For queries related to Community Welfare Cell, contact the Overall Coordinators,
Saarthak Shrivastva and Shivangi Singh

Speak in your language

The participants have to send submissions that describe their feelings or reaction to any of the given prompts. It can be a caricature or an animation, a song or a music piece, a dance video or poetry, any medium of expression you like.

Case study challenge

The 'Case study challenge' will give students an opportunity to stretch their imagination and come up with innovative solutions to the day to day challenges faced by organisations in the social sector
